Western Wear

Western wear is a clothing style derived from 19th century American old west region. Jeans, t-shirts, and jackets are the prevalent clothing forms of this style among men and women. This category of clothes is widely accepted by many cultures, as they offer comfort, durability, and variety.

Ethnic Wear

Ethnic wear is a fundamental piece of Indian culture during the celebrations. Outfits such as dhoti-kurta, ghagra-choli, kurta-pyjama, salwar suit, and saree are some of the most well-known and unmistakable ethnic wear that no Indian could ever miss during celebrations or special events.
